History of Majorville


    Following the U.S, Civil War, John Major settled in Northern Michigan In late 1854 he purchased 122 acres from a couple in Saline, MI Major built a home in this new area and planted black locust trees and an apple orchard In the late 1850s Dr. Arthur O'Leary began buying up large tracts of forest to build a sawmill which started up in 1869 producing white pine lumber and kickstarting the town of Majorville.
